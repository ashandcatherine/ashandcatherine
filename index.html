The venue

Situated between Reading and Newbury, Ufton Court is a stunning manor house set against picturesque grounds and woodland. We will be getting married in the tithe barn.

Transport

Driving

If you are planning to drive to the venue there should be no problem parking. For those using a Sat Nav, the postcode is RG7 4HD.

Public transport links

If you are not driving, there aren't many options for buses or trains direct to the venue. Instead we reccomend booking a taxi.
