A thousand paper cranes

An ancient Japanese legend promises that anyone who folds a thousand origami cranes will be granted a wish by the Gods. We will be making as many paper cranes for the big day and invite you to bring along yours too.

Here's how to make your own.

The venue

Situated between Reading and Newbury, Ufton Court is a stunning manor house set against picturesque grounds and woodland. We will be getting married in the tithe barn. Both the ceremony and reception will take place at Ufton Court.

The bar is cash only and there are no cash machines on site.
